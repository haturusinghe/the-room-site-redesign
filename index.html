The Room

Synopsis:

Johnny is a successful banker with great respect for and dedication to the people in his life, especially his future wife Lisa. Johnny can also be a little too trusting at times, which haunts him later. His beautiful blonde fiancee Lisa has always gotten her way and will manipulate to get what she wants. She is a taker, with a double personality, and her deadly schemes lead to her own downfall. Mark is Johnny's successful, independent best friend; he has a good heart, but he gets caught up in Lisa's dangerous web and gives in to temptation. This eventually brings him to great loss."The Room" depicts the depths of friendship and relationships in life and raises life's real and most-asked question:

Can anyone ever be trusted ?
